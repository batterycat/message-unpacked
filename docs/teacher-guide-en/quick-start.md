# Quick start

## Before class

- Use a projector for teacher-led modes.
- For self-paced or live interaction, confirm that learners can use a phone,
  tablet, or computer and can open the public site on the school network.
- The English demo is fixed to grades 10–12. Choose a topic and 10, 20, or 30
  minutes; the site recommends 2, 4, or 6 cases.
- Review every selected case. Add, remove, or replace cases before starting.
- Read the [pilot safety notice](sensitive-content.md) and adjust the selection
  for your class.

## Choose a delivery path

Use a **static activity link** when learners can answer independently or the
class can share one projected screen. Static links need no classroom backend
and do not transmit answers.

Use **live classroom interaction** when you want anonymous class-level answer
counts and distributions. The teacher projects the full case; learner phones
show only the number and choices.

## Test once

1. Open the generated link or room on the teacher device.
2. Join from a second device or private browser window.
3. Complete one case, reveal the explanation, and verify that official links
   are reachable from the school network.
4. Keep a static projector link ready as the fallback for live mode.
