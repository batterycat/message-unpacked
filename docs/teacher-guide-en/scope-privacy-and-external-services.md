# Scope, privacy, and external services

Message, Unpacked. teaches general verification habits through prepared cases.
It does not decide whether a learner's real message is genuine and does not
replace police, legal, financial, counselling, or school professional judgment.

## Do not submit real messages or personal data

Do not paste or upload real messages, suspicious URLs, credentials, payment
details, or another person's information into an issue, case file, classroom
screen, GitBook search, AI assistant, page-feedback form, or future project
form.

Static activities do not ask for names, accounts, phone numbers, or rosters.
Answers and progress remain in the current page. Live interaction is a separate
optional mode using a random short-lived participation credential and the
current answer only; it creates no roster, ranking, grade, or cross-question
individual history.

## External services

The demo site uses GitHub Pages, the guide uses GitBook, and the optional live
room service uses Cloudflare Workers and Durable Objects. These providers may
process technical data such as IP addresses, browser details, requested pages,
service logs, abuse signals, and recovery copies under their own policies.

GitBook search, an AI assistant, or page-rating features may additionally send
the query, prompt, or rating entered by a reader to GitBook. Teachers and
learners must not enter real messages or personal data into those controls. The
project recommends keeping optional AI-assistant and page-rating features
disabled unless their classroom purpose and privacy notice have been reviewed.

- [GitHub Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement)
- [GitBook Privacy Statement](https://gitbook.com/docs/policies/privacy-and-security/statement)
- [GitBook cookie information](https://gitbook.com/docs/policies/privacy-and-security/statement/cookies)
- [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/)

The project does not receive individual student answer history from GitHub
Pages or GitBook. Material changes to hosting, analytics, accounts, message
analysis, rosters, grades, or persistent answer storage require a new privacy
review and updated notice.
